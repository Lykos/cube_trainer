• The main part of the cube explorer are the Sessions. Here you can train commutators or other blind related alg sets. You can also find this by clicking on the Sessions tab on the top.
  • You can set up a session by clicking here or on the New button in the sessions tab. You will go through a flow where you can set up the right alg set, cube size etc.
  • In the Method Explorer you can calculate the average number of algs needed depending on which alg sets you know. You can also find this by clicking on the Method Explorer tab on the top.
  • You can change your settings (like your color scheme and letter scheme), check your achievements and notifications and change your password here. You can also find this by clicking your user name on the top right.
  • In the About page you can read how CubeTrainer was developed. You can also find this by clicking on About in the footer.
  • Here you can find out how to contact the author or file bugs. You can also find this by clicking on Contact in the footer.
